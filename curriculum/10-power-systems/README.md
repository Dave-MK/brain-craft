# 10 — Power Systems

**Mission:** Understand Energy (Mission 6)

## Why this comes late, deliberately

Per the original scoping conversation this repo is built from: you can go surprisingly far into AI-for-energy work before needing deep specialist electrical-engineering knowledge. This folder adds the domain grounding (how grids actually behave) *after* the learner already has the computational tools to model it, rather than gating everything behind an electrical engineering degree upfront.

## Capstone contribution

Digital twin fidelity — turning the simplified simulated grid used in earlier missions into a realistic one (load flow basics, generation mix, transmission constraints, renewable intermittency).

## Learning objectives

- How electricity grids actually work: generation, transmission, distribution, balancing
- Load and generation characteristics (including renewable intermittency) at a level sufficient to model, not to design real hardware
- Grid stability and failure modes (why overloads/cascading failures happen)
- Energy markets and pricing basics, enough to model price signals realistically
